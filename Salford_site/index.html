Salford university

BEACON APP

My Salford university beacon app offers a great way to engage students and staff with personalised, highly contextual BEACON APP messages. The App has a comprehensive classes modules and a great mobile tour, with ability of access to campuses, maps, dining.

The challenge


Desap discussed with University of Salford how we could send proximiy-based notification to mobile devices with a mobile app. The mobile application should provide more engagement between students and staff with digital solutions and try to deliver more value within the university experience. Also we wondered if students and lecturers could have a digital ID cards on their smart phones.

  • Navigation that directs user to campuses within the University
  • Classes time table with reminders
  • Promotional offers
  • Digital student card

The Solution

My University Salford beacon app is a mobile app that helps students and staff in a convenient way to get more information about different modules. direction to all campuses within the university, receive all events and promotional offers.
